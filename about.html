About Us

Smart Advisor is dedicated to empowering Tanzanian farmers with accurate and actionable climate predictions, helping them navigate the complexities of an ever-changing environment. By providing timely and reliable weather forecasts, we enable farmers to make informed decisions that enhance crop yields, reduce risks, and improve overall agricultural productivity. Our approach is rooted in a deep understanding of local conditions, allowing us to offer tailored solutions that address the specific challenges faced by farmers in different regions of Tanzania.

Our commitment extends beyond just providing data; we are passionate about driving positive change in the agricultural sector. We believe that by equipping farmers with the right tools and knowledge, we can contribute to food security, economic stability, and sustainable farming practices. Through continuous innovation and collaboration with local communities, we aim to create a platform that not only supports farmers in their daily operations but also builds resilience against the increasing threats of climate change. We are more than just a service provider; we are a partner in the journey towards a more sustainable and prosperous future for Tanzanian agriculture. What we offer

Smart Climate Advisor offers a comprehensive suite of climate prediction services tailored to meet the needs of Tanzanian farmers, who face significant challenges from drought and floods. By providing accurate, real-time weather updates and long-term seasonal climate forecasts, we help farmers plan their activities more effectively, reducing the risks associated with unpredictable weather patterns.


In addition to weather forecasts, our platform offers expert agricultural advisories, guiding farmers on optimal crop choices, planting schedules, and resource management. One of the core features of our service is the early warning system for extreme weather events, particularly droughts and floods. These alerts enable farmers to take proactive measures to safeguard their crops and livestock, ensuring better resilience against adverse conditions.
